# Bahrain Premium Deals - Business Plan

## Executive Summary

**Vision:** Become Bahrain's #1 platform for discovering premium product discounts, reducing food waste while helping consumers save money on quality items.

**Mission:** Connect Bahraini shoppers with real-time deals on premium products across major supermarkets, building a community-driven platform that benefits both consumers and retailers.

**Target Launch:** Q2 2026 (Pilot with 1-2 stores)
**Target Market:** Bahrain residents, 150,000+ smartphone users
**Revenue Model:** Store partnerships, featured deals, premium subscriptions

---

## Market Analysis

### Market Size
- **Bahrain Population:** ~1.5 million
- **Smartphone Penetration:** 95%+
- **Monthly Supermarket Shoppers:** ~400,000 households
- **Target Audience:** 50,000-100,000 active users within 12 months

### Major Supermarkets in Bahrain
1. **Lulu Hypermarket** - Largest chain, 6+ locations
2. **Al Jazira Supermarket** - Local favorite, premium selection
3. **Carrefour** - International brand, City Centre location
4. **Alosra Supermarket** - Established local chain
5. **Geant** - Avenues Mall location
6. **Al Osra** - Neighborhood stores

### Customer Pain Points
- Premium products often discounted unpredictably
- Yellow sticker deals found by chance, not systematically
- No central platform to compare deals across stores
- Food waste from products approaching expiry
- Limited time to visit multiple stores

### Competitive Landscape
- **No direct competitors** in Bahrain for this specific niche
- General coupon apps exist but lack real-time store deals
- Social media groups share deals manually (WhatsApp, Instagram)
- Too Good To Go operates in other markets but not Bahrain

## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Pilot Launch (Month 1-3)
**Objective:** Validate concept with early adopters

**Tactics:**
1. **Soft launch** with friends, family, tech community
2. **University partnerships** - target students (price-sensitive)
3. **Social media** - Instagram, TikTok showing deal finds
4. **WhatsApp groups** - existing deal-sharing communities
5. **Influencer seeding** - 3-5 local food/lifestyle influencers

**Goal:** 1,000 users, 50+ daily active users

### Phase 2: Store Partnership (Month 4-6)
**Objective:** Secure first store partner

**Approach to Lulu Hypermarket:**
1. **Data presentation:**
   - Show pilot user metrics
   - Demonstrate engagement rates
   - Present food waste reduction potential

2. **Pitch benefits:**
   - Increased foot traffic during slow hours
   - Reduced waste disposal costs
   - Customer loyalty enhancement
   - CSR: sustainability angle
   - Data insights on customer preferences

3. **Partnership proposal:**
   - Pilot with 1-2 locations
   - 3-month trial period
   - No upfront costs
   - Revenue share on attributed sales (5-10%)

4. **Success metrics:**
   - Track app-driven store visits
   - Measure redemption rates
   - Monitor social media sentiment
   - Calculate waste reduction

### Phase 3: Growth (Month 7-12)
**Objective:** 50,000 users, 3+ store partners

**Channels:**
1. **PR & Media:**
   - Local newspapers (Gulf Daily News, Tribune)
   - TV segments (Bahrain TV morning shows)
   - Radio spots during commute hours

2. **Digital Marketing:**
   - Facebook/Instagram ads (geo-targeted)
   - Google Ads (keywords: "Bahrain deals", "discount")
   - TikTok viral content (deal unboxing)

3. **Partnerships:**
   - Benefit pay integration
   - Bank credit card partnerships (cashback offers)
   - Corporate employee benefits programs

4. **Community Building:**
   - "Deal Hunter" badges/gamification
   - User leaderboards (most deals found)
   - Monthly prize draws

## Revenue Model

### Revenue Streams (Year 1-2)

#### 1. Store Commission (Primary - 60%)
- **Model:** 5-10% commission on sales attributed to app
- **Tracking:** QR codes, unique discount codes
- **Projected:** BHD 5,000-10,000/month by Month 12

#### 2. Featured Deal Placements (30%)
- **Model:** Stores pay to feature deals at top
- **Pricing:** BHD 50-200 per featured placement per week
- **Projected:** BHD 2,000-4,000/month by Month 12

#### 3. Premium Subscription (10%)
- **Model:** BHD 2.99/month for premium features
- **Features:**
  - Early access to deals (15 min before public)
  - Unlimited saved favorites
  - Priority notifications
  - Ad-free experience
- **Target:** 500 subscribers by Month 12 = BHD 1,500/month

#### 4. Future Revenue (Year 2+)
- Affiliate marketing partnerships
- Sponsored content from brands
- Data insights reports for retailers
- White-label solution for other GCC markets

### Financial Projections

**Year 1:**
- Revenue: BHD 50,000-80,000
- Costs: BHD 40,000-60,000
  - Development: BHD 20,000
  - Marketing: BHD 15,000
  - Operations: BHD 5,000-10,000
  - Legal/Admin: BHD 5,000
- Net: Break-even to +BHD 20,000

**Year 2:**
- Revenue: BHD 200,000-300,000
- Net profit: BHD 100,000-150,000

## Legal & Compliance

### Bahrain Requirements
1. **Commercial Registration (CR)**
   - Register with Ministry of Industry and Commerce
   - Choose entity type (WLL recommended)
   - Cost: ~BHD 500-1,000

2. **Data Protection (PDPL Compliance)**
   - Comply with Bahrain PDPL
   - Privacy policy
   - Data storage protocols
   - User consent mechanisms

3. **Terms of Service**
   - User-generated content policy
   - Liability disclaimers
   - Dispute resolution

4. **Intellectual Property**
   - Trademark registration
   - Copyright protection
   - Brand guidelines

5. **Store Partnership Agreements**
   - Master service agreements
   - Revenue sharing terms
   - Data sharing protocols
   - Termination clauses

## Risk Analysis

### Technical Risks
- **App store rejection:** Mitigate with compliance review
- **Scalability issues:** Use cloud infrastructure
- **Data breaches:** Implement security best practices

### Business Risks
- **Store reluctance to partner:** Start with data-driven pilot
- **Low user adoption:** Focus on marketing and UX
- **Competitor entry:** Build strong store relationships early
- **Economic downturn:** Premium deals more attractive in recession

### Operational Risks
- **Deal accuracy:** Implement verification system
- **User fraud:** Manual review + AI detection
- **Seasonal fluctuations:** Diversify deal categories

### Mitigation Strategies
1. Start small with pilot
2. Build strong store relationships
3. Focus on community trust
4. Maintain lean operations
5. Diversify revenue streams
